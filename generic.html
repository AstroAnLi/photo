About


Growing up in Colorado inspired An's awe for the outdoors. Now, she is a dual-title Ph.D. candidate in Earth and Space Sciences with Astrobiology at the University of Washington. She studies how glaciers shaped the landscape of Mars and their analog environments on Earth. Find out more about her research here: anliscience.com. She aims to combine her scientific background with her 14-year long journey in photography to share the beauty of our planet.
